The Dream Team

Our Culinary Visionaries

Combining decades of global experience to bring you unparalleled dining journeys.

Marco Pierre

Executive Chef & Co-Founder

Chef Marco brings over two decades of culinary mastery forged in Michelin-starred establishments across Paris and London. He believes that true gastronomy is rooted in respecting the seasons and unlocking the pure, natural flavor profiles of high-quality local ingredients.

Specialty: Modern French Fusion & Fine Dining Architecture

Elena Rostova

Head Pastry Architect

Elena views dessert making as a delicate science blended with absolute abstract art. Having graduated top of her class at Le Cordon Bleu, she transforms simple chocolate, sugar, and organic fruits into stunning edible sculptures that deliver a harmonious balance of texture and sweetness.

Specialty: High-End Pastry, Artisan Soufflés & Sugar Blowing

Antonio Rossi

Saucier & Flame Master

Hailing straight from Tuscany, Antonio is a master of fire and reduction. He manages our premium wood-fire grill grills with incredible precision, ensuring every cut of Wagyu and Angus beef reaches optimum tenderness while crafting complex, savory signature sauces that have defined ATA's legacy.

Specialty: Charcoal Grilling Techniques & Infused Emulsion Sauces

Sarah Jenkins

Sous Chef / Kitchen General

Sarah acts as the heartbeat of our kitchen ecosystem. Known for her impeccable speed, unmatched focus, and deep logistics expertise, she oversees menu execution, daily fresh inventory checks, and ensures that every plate leaving the counter meets ATA's strict luxury standards.

Specialty: High-Volume Kitchen Logistics & Cold Kitchen Appertizers
